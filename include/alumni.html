Mindy 2019

I’m from Boston so home was just a Red Line ride away when I was at MIT! As an alum I can definitively say that I remember the people at MIT better than the academics (no one tell my professors, please), and for that I am grateful to B2 for giving me many happy memories. . . .

One of the reasons why I was able to get through stressful undergraduate times was because I always knew I had a place to go back to...B2 is a community where you can be yourself and be accepted for who you are no matter what your background is, and if you give them unconditional love you receive that back twofold.

Favorite hobbies when I was living on the floor include baking chocolate chip banana bread at midnight, playing Mario Cart, belting Don’t Stop Believing during karaoke, having the best cuddle sessions, and roaming the hall to visit other suites.

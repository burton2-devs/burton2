mentally i’m here on the chill suite fire escape

Mindy 2019

I’m from Boston so home was just a Red Line ride away when I was at MIT! As an alum I can definitively say that I remember the people at MIT better than the academics (no one tell my professors, please), and for that I am grateful to B2 for giving me many happy memories. . . .

One of the reasons why I was able to get through stressful undergraduate times was because I always knew I had a place to go back to...B2 is a community where you can be yourself and be accepted for who you are no matter what your background is, and if you give them unconditional love you receive that back twofold.

Favorite hobbies when I was living on the floor include baking chocolate chip banana bread at midnight, playing Mario Cart, belting Don’t Stop Believing during karaoke, having the best cuddle sessions, and roaming the hall to visit other suites.

Favorite moments include:
  • Spotting Avery in center suite at 2AM from my bedroom window and immediately running over to get a good hug (was in middle of pset, really needed it)
  • Helping Gwen with finals breakfasts...there is satisfaction in feeding people
  • The insanity of making three dishes for Apple Bake
  • Having #deeptalks with sober and non-sober people All the Time
  • Giving advice to frosh about not overcommitting themselves to too many things (been there, done that)
  • Bumping into Erick and walking away from that conversation having learned something new (e.g. how to buy a quality razor?? Don’t ask me now, I’ve forgotten already)
  • Getting a pixie cut with Davis
  • Trying to teaching Chloe how to flip a pancake and then splatting onto the ground
  • Announcing to the floor that waffle batter was available on a Sunday morning and seeing sleepy people file into the suite for breakfast
  • Watching Moonlight with Chloe, Rachel, and other peeps and crying
  • Watching Game of Thrones and screaming
  • Cuddling Meryl in an armchair
  • Sleeping on a couch
  • Sleeping on the beanbag
  • Sleeping in the hallway
  • Falling asleep in someone else’s bed
  • Falling asleep on Chris Madrigal’s shoulder
  • Drinking two cups of coffee and still falling asleep
  • Waking up to two mice fighting in the kitchen. That was surreal.
  • Waking up to the sunrise over the Charles river.
  • And so many more things!

We’re more than “chill”

Gwen GRA

I was the GRA for Burton 2 for two years before graduating with a math PhD in 2020 (an interesting year -- see photo…). A couple of fun facts about me: I was on my school’s diving team for a year in community college, both of my parents are violinmakers, and after Burton-Conner was closed in March of 2020, I spent five months on a farm in Idaho. . . . As a GRA, I had a great time on Burton 2, and I would definitely recommend it to new students! Feel free to send me an email if you have questions about the floor that I can answer. Some of my favorite memories of B2 include: learning the ins and outs of Kosher cooking (and spending quality time in the Kosher suites on Friday nights!), watching Jeopardy and 60s spy shows and the World Series with students from the floor, painting a collaborative mural in my apartment, feeding everyone lots of lox at finals breakfasts, and all the sweet and bittersweet moments bonding with the floor in the few days before we were evacuated in March of 2020 :’)
